How it works

Five steps from contract gaps to ongoing control.

ClearClause guides your clients through a structured, repeatable process — from first assessment to a live controls register they can manage ongoing.

1

Step 01

Your company details

We capture your company name, role, and contact details. This allows us to save your report, follow up with tailored advice, and notify you when supplier contracts change.

Step 02

Choose your supplier & industry

Select from our pilot library of cloud suppliers — Microsoft, AWS, or Google Cloud — and set your industry context so the assessment is aligned to the right regulatory framework.

Step 03

Answer context questions

A short set of questions about your data hosting, certifications required, governance obligations, and the nature of the services — so the assessment is specific to your situation, not generic.

Step 04

Receive your assessment report

A full AI-generated gap analysis: risk rating, regulatory obligations, clause-by-clause breakdown with severity levels, and the exact must-have clauses your client needs to add or negotiate.

Step 05

Generate your controls register

With one click, generate a complete ongoing controls register — mandatory and optional controls, with owner roles, frequencies, evidence requirements, and regulatory links — ready to use in your GRC tool.
