How it works

From contract review to ongoing control — in three steps.

ClearClause removes the guesswork from supplier contracting. Your clients get a structured, actionable output every time.

Step 01

Select the supplier & industry

Choose from our pilot supplier library — Microsoft, AWS, or Google Cloud — and set the industry context so the assessment aligns with the right regulatory framework, whether that's Privacy Act, APRA, ISO 27001 or others.

Step 02

Assess the public contract terms

ClearClause reads the supplier's published contract against industry-standard obligations. You get a risk rating, a gap-by-gap breakdown, and the exact clauses that need to be negotiated, added, or monitored.

Step 03

Generate an ongoing controls register

Once the assessment is complete, ClearClause generates a controls register — a structured list of what your client needs to do on a monthly, quarterly, and annual basis to manage that supplier relationship going forward.
