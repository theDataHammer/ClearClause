Frequently asked questions

Common questions.

Which suppliers are currently supported?

We currently support Microsoft, AWS, and Google Cloud as our pilot suppliers. We are expanding the library — contact us to request a specific supplier.

Is this legal advice?

No. ClearClause is a contract intelligence tool. Our assessments help identify gaps and risks, but they do not constitute legal advice. For material contracts, engage qualified legal counsel.

How are assessments kept current?

We monitor supplier contract updates and update our assessment framework when suppliers change their published terms. Advisor plan subscribers are notified of material changes.

Can I white-label this for my clients?

Yes — our Enterprise plan includes white-label options. Contact us to discuss your requirements and volume.

What data do you store?

We store your contact details and assessment inputs to deliver and improve the service. We do not sell your data. See our privacy policy for full details.

Can I request a specific industry framework?

Yes — contact us with the industry and regulatory framework you need. We regularly expand our coverage based on client requests.
